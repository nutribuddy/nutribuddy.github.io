About

NutriBuddy is currently being developed by a passionate team committed to making healthy eating simple, personalized, and accessible for everyone. Our goal is to create a natural and enjoyable journey, helping you build the discipline for lifelong fitness without the tediousness or demotivation. By leveraging the power of machine learning, NutriBuddy provides personalized meal plans tailored to your unique needs and preferences. The overall application experience is designed to require minimal effort from the user, utilizing natural language for seamless and intuitive interaction.
